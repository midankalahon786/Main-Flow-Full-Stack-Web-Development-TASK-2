Gintama

Gintama is a Japanese anime and manga series created by Hideaki Sorachi. Set in an alternate-history late Edo period, where humanity is attacked by aliens called Amanto, the story follows Gintoki Sakata, a samurai who works as a freelancer alongside his friends Shinpachi Shimura and Kagura. The series is known for its humor, which ranges from slapstick to satire, and its ability to balance comedy with serious and emotional storylines.

One Punch Man

One Punch Man is a Japanese anime and manga series created by ONE. It follows Saitama, a hero who can defeat any opponent with a single punch but becomes bored due to a lack of challenge. The series is known for its parody of superhero tropes and its blend of action and comedy.

Konosuba

Konosuba: God's Blessing on This Wonderful World! is a Japanese anime and light novel series written by Natsume Akatsuki. It follows Kazuma Satou, a high school student who is reincarnated into a fantasy world with MMORPG elements. Along with a dysfunctional party, he embarks on various comedic adventures.
